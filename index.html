Camille's Lot

Index of Exploration

Initially this project was an exploration for a place to store my work in some publically accessible way, after discovery of Dave Liepmann's work on the lessons of Edward Tufte's style, and through learning just enough HTML to crib their style. This project finally came into being .

The entymology of this project is based on my name, Camille and the mythical caste of King Arthur, Camelot, as well as a play on words; this is a place to store 'a lot'.'

Ultimately the goal of this project is three fold, to give me a space where I can coerce myself to explore the things I know I want to but don't have the time for in daily life', an exploration of front-end design, explore data science with real non-classroom data.
